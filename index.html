Seniors 65+ Phone Scripting Dashboard

Exact Copy Flow
OPENING / INTRO Step 1

Agent: Hello, my name is [Your Name] with Life Advisors. I am just following up regarding the alert we received about your life and health benefits review. Based on our review, you and your family are not yet aware about the enhanced health coverage, funeral and burial package upgrades that are currently available for you. Don’t worry, this is just a review. By updating a few details, we can check whether you may qualify for lower monthly costs, enhanced health coverage, or benefit options that better fit your current situation and long term goals.

To start, Are you currently satisfied with your life insurance and final expense coverage?

🛑 Common Rebuttals during the first 5 secs
Answering: What are you calling about?

“I am calling about the alert we received regarding your health and life insurance.”

Answering: Why are you calling me?

“Based on the recent review, it seems that you and your family are not yet aware of the recent updates and upgrades that are currently available for you.”

Answering: Who are you?

“I am [Your Name] from Health Advisors. My role is to do a benefit review and just update a few details…”

Answering: I am good with my insurance!

“Perfect. That is the reason why we are calling you. We are just doing benefit review of your current insurance. So, are you satisfied with your current health and life insurance?”

(Gather interest)

Agent: Okay. Just so I can point you toward the best options, may I ask a few quick questions?

BASIC PROFILING Step 2

• Agent: How old are you?

• Agent: Are you a tobacco user?

• Agent: Do you currently have any life insurance in place?

• Agent: What are you hoping to leave behind for your family—$10,000, $20,000, or more?

(Build need)

Agent: The reason I ask is that funeral costs today can easily run $10,000-$20,000 or more, and many families end up paying those expenses out of pocket.

QUALIFY HEALTH Step 3
(Qualify health)

Agent: To see which plans you qualify for:

• Agent: Have you had any major heart issues, cancer, stroke, or COPD in the last 2 years?

• Agent: Are you currently hospitalized, in a nursing home, or receiving hospice care?

PRESENT SOLUTION Step 4
(Present solution)

Agent: Based on what you’ve shared, it looks like you may qualify for several plans. My job is to help you compare the carriers and find the best value for your budget.

PREMIUM VALUE QUERY VERBATIM:

Agent: What monthly premium range would be comfortable for you—around $50, $75, or $100 per month?

CLOSE THE CALL Step 5
(Close)

Agent: Excellent. I have a few options that fit what you’re looking for. The next step is to review the benefits and complete a quick application if you decide one makes sense.

CLOSING CALL TO ACTION VERBATIM:

Agent: Before I transfer you, may I know who I'm speaking with?

Transfer Script Final Step
PROMPT STATEMENT:

Agent: Thank you, (Leads Name), I’m now going to bring in _____, one of our licensed specialists. I’ll stay on briefly to make the introduction smooth and easy for you.

AGENT INTRO / WARM TRANSFER VERBATIM:

Agent: Hi, ______, this is (Leads Name). We reviewed their current situation, and they’re interested in exploring affordable protection options. I’ll let you take it from here.

🛡️ Live Assistance Dashboard

Objection Handling Library
“I already have coverage.”

“That’s great. Most of my clients do. The reason they speak with me is to make sure their coverage is still enough and that they’re not overpaying. It only takes a few minutes to compare.”

If skeptical:

“That’s fair — this is exactly why we review everything carefully before recommending anything.”

If worried about cost:

“We’re not trying to create another bill — the goal is to find something comfortable and sustainable.”

If “I already talked to someone”:

“Absolutely, and this is simply a second review to make sure nothing was missed and that you’re getting the best value available.”

If “I need to think about it”:

“Of course — most people do. That’s why we at least make sure you have accurate information and real numbers first.”

If older lead:

“A lot of people requested information months ago and life just got busy. We’re simply following up to see what still makes sense for you today.”

💡 Tip: Deliver each rebuttal with a relaxed, confident tone. Validating their condition lowers their shield instantly.
